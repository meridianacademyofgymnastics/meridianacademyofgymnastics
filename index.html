Our Mission

Our goal is to encourage children of all ages to build confidence through the experience of accomplishment in sports. And to exercise young bodies and minds in a fun, safe environment while encouraging the development of self-esteem.

About Us

Meridian Academy of Gymnastics, located in the heart of Meridian, is a premier center for gymnastics instruction, dedicated to nurturing talent, fostering love for the sport, and promoting physical and mental growth. With a state-of-the-art facility, a carefully designed curriculum for all age groups, and an experienced, passionate coaching staff, we provide an unparalleled gymnastics experience. We believe in empowering each child by enhancing their strength, flexibility, coordination, and confidence through our programs. Our academy is not only a gym, it's a community, where we value and support each individual, encourage teamwork, and celebrate every achievement, big or small. At Meridian Academy, we're more than just gymnastics - we're about building the foundations for a successful future.

Competitive Team Invitation Only

Step right up, aspiring gymnastic stars! We're calling all dedicated, high-energy athletes aged 5 and up to join our exciting Competitive Gymnastics Team Program. This is where serious gymnastics meet serious fun!

This program is tailored for those ready to flip, jump, and twirl their way to gymnastics glory. We're talking local, regional, and even nation-wide competitions that will put your skills to the ultimate test from December through March. It's the chance to take your love for gymnastics from a hobby to a thrilling competitive journey.

Our program includes both the USAG Junior Olympic and USAG Xcel Programs. This means you'll get the chance to train like a pro, perfecting your vaults, nailing those uneven bars, and adding some pizzazz to your floor routines.

But don't worry, it's not all work and no play. We make sure to infuse every rigorous training session with a hefty dose of fun, team spirit, and camaraderie. Plus, you'll have our dedicated coaching staff by your side, guiding you every step, leap, and tumble of the way.

For more details on how to leap into this unique adventure with Meridian Academy's Competitive Team, please get in touch. We can't wait to help you tumble your way to the top!
